## Slide 2: The Problem

### Carbon markets are a $1.7B industry running on broken infrastructure

| Pain Point | Impact |
|-----------|--------|
| 18+ month issuance timelines | Projects run out of capital before first credit |
| Manual MRV (spreadsheets + consultants) | $50K–$150K per project in verification costs |
| Fragmented registries (Verra, Gold Standard, ACR, CAR) | No interoperability, duplicate work |
| Opaque pricing, 30–40% intermediary fees | Developers receive fraction of credit value |
| Paper-based verification | 6–12 month VVB backlogs |
| No Article 6 tooling | Countries can't meet Paris Agreement obligations |

**Bottom line:** The market is growing 25% CAGR but the infrastructure hasn't changed since 2005.

## Slide 3: The Solution

### CarbonStack — One platform for the entire credit lifecycle

```
Project Onboarding → MRV Engine → Verification → Issuance → Marketplace → Retirement
```

**What we replace:**
- 6+ disconnected tools → 1 unified platform
- 18 months → 6 months to issuance
- 30–40% fees → 8–12% transparent platform fee
- Manual processes → Automated satellite MRV + digital verification

**Key differentiator:** We don't just digitize one step — we own the full pipeline from forest floor to corporate balance sheet.

## Slide 9: Go-To-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Supply-Side (Now)
- Onboard project developers with free tier
- Target: REDD+, ARR, and cookstove projects in Global South
- Channel: Direct outreach to carbon project consultancies

### Phase 2: Demand-Side (Q4 2025)
- Activate corporate buyers through marketplace
- Target: EU companies facing CSRD reporting deadlines
- Channel: Partnerships with ESG consultancies and sustainability teams

### Phase 3: Infrastructure (2026)
- White-label for exchanges and brokerages
- Target: Regional carbon exchanges, commodity brokers
- Channel: Enterprise sales, conference presence (Carbon Forward, Climate Week)

### Wedge Strategy
Start with **project developers** (highest pain, lowest switching cost) → pull in **verifiers** (structured data makes their job easier) → attract **buyers** (quality + provenance) → lock in **network effects**.
